The material type for von Mises stress damage or yield criterion is `DC von Mises stress`. For this criterion,

\[
\Xi\left(\mathbf{F}\right)=\sqrt{\frac{3}{2}\text{dev}\boldsymbol{\sigma}:\text{dev}\boldsymbol{\sigma}}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{2}\left[\left(\sigma_{1}-\sigma_{2}\right)^{2}+\left(\sigma_{2}-\sigma_{3}\right)^{2}+\left(\sigma_{3}-\sigma_{1}\right)^{2}\right]}
\]

where $\sigma_{1}$, $\sigma_{2}$, $\sigma_{3}$ are the principal values of $\boldsymbol{\sigma}_{0}\left(\mathbf{F}\right)$.

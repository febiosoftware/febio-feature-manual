The `experimental spring` is a discrete element material that has the following force-displacement function,

\[
    f\left( d \right)=\left\{ \begin{matrix}
   {{s}_{M}}\left( 1-\exp \left( -E\,d/{{s}_{M}} \right) \right),\quad d\ge 0  \\
   -{{s}_{m}}\left( 1-\exp \left( -E\,d/{{s}_{m}} \right) \right),\quad d<0  \\
\end{matrix} \right.
\]
